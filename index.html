About Me

Hello World! My name is Tina Tran, and I am a sophomore at the University of Central Florida’s Burnett Honors College studying Computer Science and Mathematics. Ever since I learned about Alpha Go in high school, I have been interested in learning more about Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. This passion has been all the more fueled by the recent explosion of AI-related media due to ChatGPT.

Oftentimes others comment on my insatiable thirst for knowledge as the main reason for the multitude of various involvement I have done. However, I believe everything is only possible due to skills in time management, discipline, and perseverance I learned from my extracurricular activities. In addition, I have learned multiple computer languages when working on projects including Golang, SQL, JavaScript, Java, Python, and C as well as the PyTorch framework for Machine Learning. These skills will benefit me in my research career.

Research

I am currently conducting research in the department of Mathematics with Dr. Carlos Borges in connecting Reinforcement Learning with the Inverse Scattering Problem. I also am developing a Honors Undergraduate Thesis with Dr. Rickard Ewetz in Machine Learning with music data.

Projects

As a computer science major, I also love to program. I am involved in many projects both in academics and outside. I have a leading role in the student-led Multus Medium, where we are developing a multimedia recommendation system. At my summer internship at Snap One, I worked on gathering data using SQL queries to develop a documentation website for all the events within the Rebase data lake. I also work on various personal projects in my spare time with my twin sister, another computer science student at UCF. One such project I have coded is this very website using the Hyperspace template from HTML5 UP!

Extracurricular

Outside of academics, I am an active Kung Fu student taking online lessons. I am a brown-belt studying the Mi Zhong Luo Han style, which is within the Northern Shaolin category of Kung Fu. I also play the trumpet, though currently I am taking a break to focus on academics.
