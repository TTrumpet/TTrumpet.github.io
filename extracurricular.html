Kung Fu

I am a brown-belt Kung Fu student at Tang Martial Arts Center located in Bradenton, Florida. I study under my master, Sifu Leng Tang. I study the Mi Zhong Luo Han style, a Northern Shaolin Mi Zong branch which belongs to the “long-fist” Northern group of Kung Fu similar to Tai Chi, Pah Kwa, and Tan Tui. Mi Zhong Luo Han was first developed by Grand Master Ye Yu-Ting, alias Chuk-Chuan Ye, from the Chuong Hsien of Hopeh Provence in Northern China and was born in the times of Emperor Kwang Shui of the Ching Dynasty. This style of Kung Fu is characterized by the versatility of hand and foot movements which lead to fleeting footwork and nimble jumps.

Trumpet

I am a trumpet player who started in middle school, 7th grade (August 2017).
